5-Minute Mocha Pots

  1. Melt the chocolate in the microwave for 2 mins, stirring halfway through, or over a pan of gently simmering water. Leave to cool a little.
  2. Using an electric whisk, whip the double cream with the vanilla in a bowl until lightly whipped. Fold in the cooled, melted chocolate until fully cooked.
  3. Split the mixture between four small bowls or ramekins and serve topped with a dollop of crème fraîche. If you aren't serving right away, chill in the fridge and add the crème fraîche just before bringing to the table.
